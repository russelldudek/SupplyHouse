AI Fit-to-Work Review

SupplyHouse · Independent working-session artifact by Russell Dudek
Illustrative worksheet

Start with the job. Earn the install.

Use this page to decide whether a workflow should enter a bounded pilot, discovery, repair, hold or human-led disposition. It is not a maturity score.

Workflow / team

Business owner

Employee users

Review date / decision forum

1 · Job signal

What work is happening? What repeated friction or consequence makes change worthwhile? What should remain unchanged?

2 · Context + data

Which sources support the work? Who owns them? What freshness, permissions, definitions and failure modes matter?

3 · AI role

Should AI retrieve, summarize, classify, draft, recommend or act? What is explicitly out of bounds?

4 · Human authority

Who approves, overrides, escalates and supports? What happens when evidence is weak or the system fails?

5 · Proof + reuse

Which workflow, quality, reliance, adoption, confidence, access and economic evidence will drive the next decision?

Enablement + change burden

What must employees and managers do differently? What practice, support, communication and career pathway are required?

Disposition

COMMISSIONBounded pilot with named owner, authority and evidence.
DISCOVER / REPAIRValue may exist; resolve context, design, control or adoption gaps.
HOLDReadiness or evidence is insufficient for responsible pilot work.
HUMAN-LED / STOPAI does not improve this work enough to justify the change burden.
